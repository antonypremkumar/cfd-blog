According to Versteeg, Computational fluid dynamics or CFD is the analysis of systems involving fluid flow, heat transfer and associated phenomena such as chemical reactions by means of computer-based simulation.

The Basics

CFD tries to solve a initial-boundary value problem a partial differential equation. This equation is called the Navier Stokes equation.
